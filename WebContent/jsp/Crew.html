Crew Members
Information for Crew Members, Family, and Friends
ATTENTION ALL HANDS... NOW HEAR THIS!

Hello from Jan Humphries! I am the designated point of contact for our veterans and their family members who attend our yearly event. It continues to be our great joy, pleasure, and utmost honor to invite USS Cowpens CVL25 and CG63 crewmembers and their families to join us in Cowpens, SC, for the 33rd Annual Mighty Moo Reunion & Festival. For those who have been with us in the past, we're so glad you are coming back! For those who have never had the chance to come experience the Moo, we're waiting on you!

Hotel Accommodations

The USS Cowpens Reunion Association makes it headquarters at the Radisson Hotel & Suites located at 9027 Fairforest Rd., Spartanburg, SC 29301 (located on Exit 2C on Business I-85). Their phone number is 864-574-2111 or 1-800-333-3333. To visit the hotel website directly, click on http://www.radisson.com/spartanburgsc. Be sure to let them know you are with the Cowpens Moo Reunion when reservations are made. The room rate is $64.00/night for either 2 Queen beds or 1 King bed. These are nice rooms with a coffee pot, hair dryer, and an ironing board. An added benefit is the elevator, so there are no stairs to climb.

Weekend Activities

The Cowpens Mighty Moo festival includes many enjoyable events. Most events are held at our beautiful Veterans Park. Next to it is the Historic Depot where the USS Cowpens Museum is housed; it will be open Friday and Saturday during the event for visitors to enjoy. Carnival rides will be running for families to enjoy three nights. Crewmembers will participate in the annual business meeting, election of reunion officers, a memorial service, and the ladies auxiliary auction on Thursday, June 17. That is also the evening of the Miss Cowpens Beauty Pageant.

On Friday morning, June 18th, the USS Cowpens crew will play a local little league team in the annual baseball game. Crew members and families are also invited to attend a morning brunch at the Bailey Home and the great Adopt-a-Crew dinner that evening. Friday morning is also the day for the Mighty Moo Golf Tournament which provides a yearly scholarship to a graduating senior at our high school.

Saturday, June 19th, features the Moo parade, entertainment, arts & crafts, food, festivities and fun. The night ends with a huge street dance held at the Cowpens Veterans Park, from where you'll be able to see the spectacular fireworks display.

The reunion association also wraps up their yearly event with a closing banquet buffet at the Radisson on Saturday night. The cost is $30 per adult and $15 per child ages 10 & under. If you are interested in attending the reunion association dinner, please send payment to

  • Warner Robins, GA 31088.
  • Reservations should be made for the banquet by June 1st.

Contact Information

Anyone attending for the first time and is not familiar with all the many activities planned for the crew and their families is invited to contact me at cowtownjan@bellsouth.net. We are excitedly anticipating the attendance of several current crewmembers serving onboard the CG63 stationed in Japan. Our community extends wide opened arms and hearts to you all and hopes to have the chance to meet more of the outstanding, courageous, heroic men and women who have served onboard the two finest ships in the United States Navy. Be prepared to be spoiled rotten and leave our hometown knowing that you've found ties with family and friends which will last a lifetime.

There's nothing left for you to do but come on and join the festivities. We'll be watching for you!
