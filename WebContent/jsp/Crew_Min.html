2012 Information for Crew Members, Family, and Friends
ATTENTION ALL HANDS... NOW HEAR THIS!

Our ship is home... our ship is home! The USS Cowpens CG63 is back stateside now residing in San Diego after 10+ years of superior service in Japan. The threat of decommissioning is no longer a worry for the near future... thank you very much!

To all crew members and family members with service on our ships during WWII or in the past 24 years on the CG63 – we encourage you to make time to come join us in Cowpens for the 36th Annual Mighty Moo Festival & Reunion. I can "guar-an-tee" you've never experienced anything like it.

Any questions from crew or family members can be directed to the Mighty Moo Reunion Veteran’s Point of Contact – Jan Humphries, at 864-542-6489 or at cowtownjan@bellsouth.net.

Just one question for you now - "Got Moo? Come get it in Cowpens!"
